About Us


          DONATE, India’s most trusted NGO, has been working towards creating happier and healthier childhoods for the last 42 years. Thanks to the support of people like you, we’ve impacted over 3 million children in India. But we have miles to go and need your help. Out of 472 million children in India today, many are still unable to go to school, do not get proper nutrition or healthcare and are vulnerable to abuse and exploitation. Donate to DONATE and give children a happy childhood they deserve. Your small contribution will make a big difference in their lives!
